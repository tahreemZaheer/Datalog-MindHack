Return & Exchange Policy

General Return Policies

  • The shopping receipt is to be kept safe as you will be required to bring it along for the purpose of Return & Exchange.
  • Return or exchange is only allowed within the first 14 days of shopping.
  • Cash will not be refunded against the returned item; instead a credit voucher of the same amount will be issued which can be used to purchase anything from any of our stores.
  • Credit voucher can be redeemed within 40 days of its issuance date.
  • Items sold under promotional or discounted prices are not exchangeable.
  • Fresh, Frozen, Pharmacy, Jewelry, Crockery, Cosmetics, Undergarments, Toys, Sprays & all Electronic items cannot be returned or exchanged.
